FAQs and Safety

In this section we will cover frequently asked questions, safetly, and even recommend some gear for you!

What terrain will we skate on?

In all of our meetups we will be skating on smooth concrete or tarmac which are great for learning. You wont be expected to skate on anything gravely, go off road, or skate along a trail, so don't worry!

Can I come alone?

Yes! Everyone in the group is incredibly welcoming and we love to see newbies. We're a social bunch and very inviting, we can also help with teaching you some basics!

How do I stay safe?

We always recommend wearing a full set of safetly gear when you are learning to Skate. This includes kneepads, elbowpads, and a set of wrist guards. You can also wear a helmet if you choose. This items are designed to protect you when you fall and are mandatory for coming to the meetups, so make sure to pick yours up here
