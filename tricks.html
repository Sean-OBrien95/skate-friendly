Your Introduction to tricks!

In this section we'll be showing you some of the basic tricks to get you started! We know learning tricks can seem like an intimidating task when you're only starting out, which is why we've selected the below ones very carefully, all of these tricks have been designed with beginners in mind are great to try once you've found your balance on the skate!

The Bubble

The bubble is a great first trick to learn as it teaches you the basics of how your feet and legs are supposed to move in the skates. You want to start this move by having your feet point outwards ar a 90 degree angel. Once there, you want to push your legs outwards, and once you reach full extension, guide your feet back inwards, completing the bubble.
