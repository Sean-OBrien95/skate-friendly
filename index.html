What Skate Friendly is All About

Our Goal

Skate Friendly is a community group that is focused around people just starting their skate journey. We are a group that skates across various parts of Dublin, and will be your guide in beginners tricks, safety, and recommended products!

Become a confident skater!

At he end of the day we want everyone to feel good in what their doing, and the best way to do this is increase your confidence! We're here to help you improve how you feel about skates and get you up to an intermediate level. You can read more about this in our Tricks section.

Benefits of Skating

Skating has plenty of health benefits, from being healthy for your heart, improving balance and flexibility, increasing strength especially in your legs and hips, and is even known to increase self-esteem! These are just a few of many health benefits which you'll discover on your skating journey.
